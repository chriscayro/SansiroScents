BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Sansiro Scents is a locally based enterprise operating in Kenya and Uganda. It was founded in August 2017.

It distributes Sansiro pen perfumes , pocket perfumes, fragrance mists and auto perfumes to mainly the young,upcoming,corporate millenials- who find socialization a big deal.

The desire to wear different designer perfumes for different occasions is strong in this target group.....and ofcourse at an affordable price.

The value add is the price and portability- easy to carry along your favourite scents, ready to use depending on where you find yourself.

We are currently online with customers across the region eg Uganda, Kenya and South Sudan.
